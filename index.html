Wage-Price Spiral

Wage Price spiral is a phenomena which happens when there happens an acute labour shortage or this type of disruptions . So, in this due less labour companies are forced to either pay there existing employees more to stay or they have to fight with other employers in term of salaries to get employees and it has resulted in companies to raise the prices of goods or services that they offer leading to more profit to afford higher salaries. But , problem with this is that it is an endless spiral fueling the inflation even more higher than it already is . So, this is the situation in a nutshell right now.
